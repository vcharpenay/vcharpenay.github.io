A Schema for What?

Relational databases always rely on a schema for storing and querying relations. Definition of schema: structure + functionality constraints + integrity constraints
Object-oriented models also rely on a schema to . Definition of schema: structure (frame) + runtime constraints
Schema.org is a schema... But no structure is enforced and no constraints apply. Difference between validation and inference. Difference between a priori and a posteriori structure. Difference between closed-world and open-world semantics.
