Agile Development Is Iterative

Agile Development Is Decentralized

An agile team has (theoretically) no upper management. It is self-organized.

International ~ Decentralized

Not limited to software artifacts or source code. Any (textual) entity can be produced in an agile fashion. Condition for success: no exchange of goods, only an exchange of information.
