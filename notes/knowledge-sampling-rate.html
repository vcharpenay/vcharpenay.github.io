The Sampling Rate of Knowledge

January 2021

Was reading a paper about Edison, a naive physics engine. Reminds me of lumped representation. One can describe a full system with various granularities. Every primitive function is given a name and composed with other functions (recursively). Such a system representation, can be sampled in any way, every sample is also a system. What is the Nyquist-Shannon sampling rate starting from which no information about the original system is lost? Applications of such research: method for learning system representations from sensor/actuator reading. Acceleration of existing learning techniques by providing simplifed (but strictly more generic) system representations
