Quantity vs. Usefulness in Data Growth

January 2022

Data generation grows exponentially. Data compression grows exponentially (compression ratios grow in log(n), where n is the size of uncompressed data). So, here is a scientific question: which one does grow faster? If data generation, we are producing useless data. If data compression, we are indeed, expanding Cyberspace, humanity's information space. How to measure these? With archive.org?
