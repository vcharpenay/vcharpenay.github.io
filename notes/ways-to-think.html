Thinking of Ways to Think

May 2020

Idea of Marvin Minksy: "Ways to Think" and hierarchical thinking (with critics/selectors). See p. 226-227 of The Emotion Machine. Experiments in psychology: model theory and reasoning: the result is "reasoners" implement different strategies. Identified 5 Ways to Think (or kinds of reasoning). Is there a selection algorithm for these different ways to think? Is the selection problem decidable? If so, there should be heuristics to help automated reasoners to perform (much) better.
